# Patent Pledge

Luke Hayden ("the Pledgor") is the applicant of Australian provisional patent
application **2026906417** (filed 19 July 2026) and any patents or applications
worldwide claiming priority from it (together, "the Pledged Patents").

**The Pledge.** The Pledgor irrevocably pledges not to assert any claim of the Pledged
Patents against any party for making, using, selling, or distributing an
implementation of the Witness Protocol (VINC-0001) or of the temporal consistency
scoring method it describes.

**Defensive termination.** This pledge terminates, only as to a specific party, if that
party (or its affiliates) initiates a patent infringement claim against the Pledgor or
against any implementer of the Witness Protocol relating to the protocol.

**Purpose.** The Pledged Patents exist to keep this protocol open — to prevent any
party, including the Pledgor, from enclosing it. Neutral infrastructure cannot carry a
toll gate.

**Note.** This pledge is published in good faith and drafted without legal counsel; a
formally settled version reviewed by a patent attorney will replace it in a future
revision without narrowing its scope.

— Luke Hayden, Melbourne, 2026
